About TerraQuest

TerraQuest is a mobile, story-driven game where players embark on a journey guided by a group of benevolent aliens. Through engaging visual novel-style dialogues and branching narratives, players navigate simulations of real-life scenarios like pollution and global warming, making impactful decisions that affect the outcome. The game also features a futuristic academy where AI companions provide scientifically backed insights to help players make informed choices.

Features

  • Story-Driven Experience: Visual novel-style dialogues with branching narratives.
  • Simulation Scenarios: Real-life issues like pollution and global warming.
  • AI NPC School: AI companions provide fact-checked educational resources.
