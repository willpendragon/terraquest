About TerraQuest

Micaela Romanini

Micaela is a trailblazer in the realm of diversity and inclusion. She has dedicated her life to advocating for equity within the tech and gaming sectors. As the founder of Women in Games Italia, she has led initiatives to address gender disparities. With a background in Business Development, Marketing, and Communications, Micaela has worked with organizations like Microsoft, the EU Commission, and EIT Culture and Creativity. At VIGAMUS, she served as Vice Director, developing business strategies and overseeing educational and marketing activities.
